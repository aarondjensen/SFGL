SFGL

Shark Fantasy Golf League

SFGL is a private fantasy golf application for the members of the Shark Fantasy Golf League — a small, invitation-only league. Members draft and manage teams of professional golfers, set weekly lineups, make roster moves, and track standings and earnings across the PGA TOUR season.

What the app does

  • Sign in securely with Google or Apple to access your team.
  • Set lineups, make add/drops and waiver claims, and view standings.
  • Track team and player earnings across the season.
  • Receive optional push notifications for league activity.

Support

SFGL is maintained for league members. If you’re a member and need help — trouble signing in, a question about your team, or a data request — reach out to the league commissioner:

email11_ask@icloud.com

Access

SFGL is a private, unlisted app. Membership is by invitation from the league commissioner, and all league data is protected behind sign-in.
