SFGL Privacy Policy

Shark Fantasy Golf League · Last updated: July 9, 2026

SFGL (“the app”) is a private, invitation-based fantasy golf application operated for the members of the Shark Fantasy Golf League. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and the choices you have. We keep this deliberately simple because the app serves a small private league and does not sell data or show advertising.

Information we collect

  • Account identity. When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name, email address, and a unique account identifier from that provider. If you use “Hide My Email” with Apple, we receive only the relayed address.
  • League data you enter. Your team name, roster and lineup selections, transactions (add/drops, waiver claims), and related league activity.
  • Push notification token. If you enable notifications, we store a device notification token so the app can send you league alerts. You can turn this off at any time.

We do not collect location data, contacts, photos, health data, or advertising identifiers, and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

How we use it

  • To sign you in and connect your account to your team.
  • To run the league — standings, rosters, transactions, and scoring.
  • To send league notifications you’ve opted into (e.g., lineup reminders, results).

How it’s stored and shared

League data and account information are stored using Google Firebase (Firestore, Firebase Authentication, and Firebase Cloud Messaging), which acts as our backend service provider. Sign-in is handled by Google and Apple. Notification emails may be sent through Brevo, an email delivery provider. These providers process data only to provide their service to the app.

Publicly available professional golf results (used for scoring) come from public sports data sources and contain no personal information about you.

We do not sell your information, share it for advertising, or provide it to anyone outside the operation of the league.

Data retention and deletion

Your information is retained while you are a member of the league. To request that your account and associated personal data be deleted, contact the league commissioner at the email below and we will remove it from the app’s database. You can disable push notifications at any time from the app’s settings, which removes your stored notification token.

Children

SFGL is intended for the adult members of a private golf league and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Access to league data requires signing in, and the app’s database is protected by authentication rules. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we’ll update the “Last updated” date above. Material changes will be communicated within the league.
